Private Tutoring

In addition to our academic services, we also provide high-quality, low-cost private tutoring for all of our classes. Private tutoring is available as a stand-alone program. We believe that connecting with a private tutor can benefit every student because they are available to address the individual needs, interests, and aspirations of each student. Learner-centered techniques such as elicitation, communicative activities, and collaborative learning are used in private tutoring to help students develop crucial speaking, listening, and writing skills.
