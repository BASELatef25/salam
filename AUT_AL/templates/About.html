Aqaba University of Technology (AUT)

AUT is a Jordanian national institution established to operate in Jordan and the region in general and Aqaba in particular. Officially accredited by the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research, Jordan. Aqaba University of Technology (AUT) offers courses and programs leading to officially recognized higher education degrees such as bachelor's degrees in several areas of study: IT, Administrative and Financial Sciences, Engineering, Pharmacy, Physical Therapy, and Medical Radiology.
