Aqaba University of Technology (AUT) is the first private university established in southern Jordan. It is located on a beautiful view of the Gulf of Aqaba. The university provides a number pf undergraduate programs accredited by The Higher Education Accreditation Commission. These programs are distributed over five faculties: - Munib and Angela Al Masri Faculty of Engineering, where the majors of civil Engineering and Architecture are taught. - Michael Sayegh Faculty of Pharmacy.
